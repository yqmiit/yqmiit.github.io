The first visualization shows the variance of planed completion data and completion data, the governors can change the timetable of the projects based on the variances shown in the visualization, find an appropriate mothed to make a plan. Both orange and blue scatter plots can be clicked, and if put mouse over the plot, it shows the specific numeric data. You also can click the plots which are on the right top of the visualization to make them disappear, if you only want to acquire the completion data or planned project completion data.

The second visualization indicates the number of projects in different department, which gives the medium business men some business idea. Because they can see the trend of development of the county as the government set up a lot of projects on these department. The elements on the right can remove and add back a bar on the left. Put you mouse on the bar, it can show the number of the project in the particular department.
